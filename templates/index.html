Zone-Based WiFi Scanner

Find the BEST spot for your WiFi router by testing different zones in your room. We'll guide you with voice and visual feedback!

Step-by-Step Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Choose between 4 zones (quick scan) or 9 zones (detailed scan)
  2. Step 2: Click "Start Zone Scan" and wait for voice instructions
  3. Step 3: Walk to each zone when told and STAND STILL for 35 seconds
  4. Step 4: Listen for feedback:
    • "WARMER" = This zone is better than the last one!
    • "COLDER" = Previous zone was better
    • Green zone = Good signal | Yellow = OK | Red = Bad
  5. Step 5: Repeat for all zones - we'll tell you the BEST spot at the end!
Pro Tip: Stand still and avoid moving during each zone scan for accurate results!
